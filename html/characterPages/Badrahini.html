Badrahini

Alternative names: Rohini

Terms of address: Dewi

Kalangan: Hapsari

Description: Descendant of Batara Bramana. Third wife of Basudewa (with Mahira/Maerah and Mahindra/Maekah). Rara Ireng (also known as Sumbadra) is her daughter.

Father: Bramana?

Spouses: Basudewa

Found in the follwing lakon : ['Semar Barang Jantur', 'Basudewa Grogol']
