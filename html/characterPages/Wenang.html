Wenang

Terms of address: Batara

Type: God

Origin: Java

Description in the Sanskrit version: Not mentioned in the Indian Mahabharata.

Description in the Javanese version: Sang Hyang Wenang. Father of Togog, Guru and Semar, with Dewi Wiranti (daughter of Rekatatama). He appears as Ruci in Dewa Ruci.

Found in the follwing lakon: Wisanggeni_Lahir, Semar_Boyong_(Wahyu_Katetreman), Suksma_Langgeng
