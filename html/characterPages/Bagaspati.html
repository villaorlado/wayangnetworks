Bagaspati

Terms of address: Begawan

Kalangan: Raksasa

Description: Also known as Prabu Nindyamaya, Anggana Putra and Prabu Mandrakeswara. Son of Batara Nagapasara and Dewi Anggini. He marries Darmastuti and has a daughter named Pujawati. He is killed by Salya (Narasoma), his son in law.
