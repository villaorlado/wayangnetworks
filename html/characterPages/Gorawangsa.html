Gorawangsa

Terms of address: Prabu

Type: Raksasa

Origin: Java

Notes on the Sanskrit version: The story of Krishna and the false Vasudeva is mentioned the /Amar Chitra Katha/ series. He is Paundraka Vasudeva, the King of Pundara. He sees Krishna as his enemy and also imitates Krishna's attire.

Description in the Javanese version: He fathers Kangsa with [Mahera] while impersonating Basudewa. King of Gowabarong.

Found in the follwing lakon (stories):

  1. Basudewa_Grogol
