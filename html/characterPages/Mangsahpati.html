Mangsahpati

Terms of address: Prabu

Type: Human

Origin: India

Description in the Sanskrit version: King Virata was the ruler of the Matsya Kingdom. The Pandavas serve him in his kingdom during their final year of exile as they would need to spend that year in disguise. His daughter Uttara marries Arjuna's son, Abhimanyu.

Description in the Javanese version: King of Wirata, also known as Durgandana. Pandawa serve him at the end of their 13-year exile.

Found in the follwing lakon: Puntadewa_Wisudha, Brajadhenta_Mbalela_(Gatotkaca_Wisudha)
