Mangsahpati

Alternative names: Matswapati, Durgandana

Terms of address: Prabu

Type: Human

Origin: India

Notes on the Indian versions: King Virata was the ruler of the Matsya Kingdom. The Pandavas served him during their final year of exile as they needed to spend that year in disguise. His daughter Uttara married Arjuna's son, Abhimanyu.

Description in the Javanese wayang versions: King of Wirata, known as Durgandana in his youth. He helped the Pandawa in numerous occasions, most notably when they were building Amarta. The Pandawa served him at the end of their 13-year exile. He died defending the Pandawa in the Baratayuda war, together with all his sons. Other versions, said he achieved perfect death an incarnated into a fish, before the beginning of the war.
