Dersanala

Terms of address: Batari

Type: God

Origin: Java

Description in the Javanese wayang retellings: Mother of Wisanggeni with Arjuna. Daughter of [Brama], who wants her to marry Dewasrani.

Notes on the Indian retellings: Not mentioned in the Indian Mahabharata.
