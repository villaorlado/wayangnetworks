Basudewa

Terms of address: Prabu

Type: Human

Origin: India

Notes on the Sanskrit version: Vasudeva is the biological father of Lord Krishna. His wife is Devaki, sister of the evil King Kamsa.

Description in the Javanese version: A central character in the Mahabharata. His sons and siblings all play major roles as well. He fathers Arya Udawa with [Ken_Sagupi] out of wedlock. He is impersonated by Gorawangsa, who then fathers Kangsa with Maerah.

Family relationships

Father: Kuntiboja

Siblings: Kunti, Bismaka, Ugrasena

Consorts: Mahindra, Maerah, Badrahini, Ken_Sagupi

Offspring: Kresna, Baladewa, Rara_Ireng, Udawa

More information

Ruler of: Mandura

Impersonated by: Gorawangsa

Sources: Mengenal Gambar Tokoh Wayang Purwa, p. 40.

Found in the follwing lakon: Semar_Barang_Jantur, Basudewa_Grogol, Kunthi_Pilih_(Lahiripun_Adipati_Karna)
