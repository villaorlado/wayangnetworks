Rama

Terms of address: Prabu (Ramuwijaya) / Raden Ramaregawa

Type: Human

Origin: India

Notes on the Sanskrit version: Rama is known as the King of Ayodhya. He is of the Solar lineage (Suryavamsa) which means descendents of the Sun god. The kingdom is ruled by King Dilipa followed by his son Rahu, Rahu's son Aja, Aja's son Dasharatha and Dasharatha's son Rama. This why he is sometimes addressed as Raghurama or Raghu Kula Rama (hence alluding his origins from Raghu lineage. He is born to Dasharatha and Kausalya, the first wife of Dasharatha.

Alternative names: Ramawijaya

Description in the Javanese version: Son of Dasarata and Kausalya.
