Nembur_Nawa

Alternative names: Nabatnawa, Nemburnawa

Terms of address: Naga

Type: Animal

Origin: India

Notes on the Indian versions: There is a story of a snake that entraps Bhima, but the context and ending of the story are very different. The snake is actually Nahusha, Bhima's ancestor who was turned into a snake by sage Agasthya. In the end Bhima helps him redeem himself and return to his previous shape.

Description in the Javanese wayang versions: The giant snake defeated by Werkudara in Dewa Ruci.

Found in the follwing stories (lakon):

  1. Dewa_Ruci
