Asmarawati

Alternative names: Mambang

Terms of address: Endang

Kalangan: Human

Description: According to Bababd Wanamarta, she is the daughter of Begawan Wilawuk. Also known as Jim Mambang, who marries Arjuna. //Another version: Wife of Sumitra (son of Arjuna and Dewi Larasati). Their marriage is an important marriage in the wayang cycle. Daughter of Suryaasmara.

Father: Wilawuk

Spouses: Arjuna

Found in the follwing lakon : ['Babad Wanamarta', 'Puntadewa Wisudha']
