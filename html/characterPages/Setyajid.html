Setyajid

Alternative names: Ugrasena

Terms of address: Raden

Type: Human

Origin: India

Notes on the Indian versions: Ugrasena is known as the father of Kamsa and Devaki and grandson of Krishna and Balarama. He is imprisoned by Kamsa when he seizes his throne.

Description in the Javanese wayang versions: Known as Ugrasena in his youth, known as Setyajid after becoming King. Once, [Kala_Ruci], a giant, was wreaking havoc in the heavens because he wanted to marry the heavenly nymph [Wresini]. With the help of Pandu, Ugrasena defeated the giant. As a reward, he was given [Wresini]'s hand in marriage. Some versions says he also fathered Pragota with [Ken_Sagopi]. But the most common version is that Pragota is the son of Saragupita.
