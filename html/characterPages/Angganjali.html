Angganjali

Alternative names: Iwaksa

Terms of address: Empu

Type: Human

Origin: Java

Description in the Javanese wayang retellings: An artist from the Medan Kamulan kingdom that make weapons for the Gods. Together with Ramayadi, he provides Tetuka (Gatotkaca as a baby) with the power to withstand attacks from Sekipu and Pracona. He was considered as one of the Gods and given the kingdom of Surati, at which point he became better known as Prabu Iwaksa.

Notes on the Indian retellings: Not mentioned in the Indian Mahabharata.
