Angganjali

Terms of address: Empu

Type: Human

Origin: Java

Notes on the Sanskrit version: Not found in the Indian Mahabharata.

Alternative names: Iwaksa

Description in the Javanese version: An artist from the Medan Kamulan kingdom that make weapons for the Gods. Together with Ramayadi, he provides Tetuka (Gatotkaca as a baby) with the power to withstand attacks from Sekipu and Pracona. He was considered as one of the Gods and given the kingdom of Surati, at which point he became better known as Prabu Iwaksa.
