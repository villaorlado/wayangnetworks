Yuda_Kala_Kresna

Terms of address: Prabu

Type: Raksasa

Origin: Java

Notes on the Sanskrit version: Not mentioned in the Indian Mahabharata.

Alternative names: Buda_Kala_Kresna, Yudha_Kala_Kresna, Kunjana_Kresna

Description in the Javanese version: An ogre that attacks the heavens to steal Setyaboma. He is a descendant of Batara Wisnu. Narayana gets his name Kresna and the kingdom of Dwarawati from this ogre after defeating him.

Found in the follwing lakon (stories):

  1. Narayana_Kridha_Brata

Family relationships

More information

Killed by: Kresna

Sources: Ensiklopedi Wayang Purwa, p. 270; Sejarah Wayang Purwa, pp. 346-347; Ensiklopedi Wayang Indonesia, pp. 176-177 (Vol. V)
