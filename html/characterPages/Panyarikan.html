Panyarikan

Terms of address: Batara

Type: God

Origin: Java

Notes on the Sanskrit version: Not mentioned in the Indian Mahabharata.

Description in the Javanese version: God of writing with colorful headdress. Never forgets and can keep a secret. Son of Sang Hyang Parma, grandson of Sang Hyang Taya, younger brother of Sang Hyang Wenang. // there are many versions. There is a version that says he is the son of Batara Guru, another has him as the son of Ismaya.

Found in the follwing lakon (stories):

  1. Narayana_Kridha_Brata
  2. Gatotkaca_Lahir
