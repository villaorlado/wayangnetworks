Durga

Alternative names: Uma, Umayi, Parwati, Pramoni, Pramuni

Terms of address: Batari

Type: God

Origin: India

Notes on the Indian versions: Known as the goddess who killed Mahishasura, a buffalo headed demon for his atrocities against rishis and deva. She is also the wife of Lord Shiva.

Description in the Javanese wayang versions: The wife of Batara Guru in her Raksasa shape. [Uma] was turned into this shape by Batara Guru for refusing to have sex with him. She was returned to her original shape by Sadewa. In some versions, she has Dewasrani with Batara [Kala], not with Batara Guru. In her shape as Uma, she has four children with Guru: Brama, Indra, Bayu and Wisnu. After Uma becomes Durga, Batara Guru inserts Uma's soul into [Laksmi], his second wife.
