Durga

Terms of address: Batari

Type: God

Origin: India

Description in the Sanskrit version: Known as the goddess who killed Mahishasura, a buffalo headed demon for his atrocities against rishis and deva. She is also the wife of Lord Shiva.

Alternative names: Uma, Parwati, Pramoni

Description in the Javanese version: Dewi Pramoni. Wife of Batara Guru. Uma is cursed to become Batari Durga.

Disguised as: _Woman

Found in the follwing lakon: Wisanggeni_Lahir, Brajadhenta_Mbalela_(Gatotkaca_Wisudha), Sudamala, Gatotkaca_Lahir, Wahyu_Kembar
