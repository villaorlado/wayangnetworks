Dhandhonwacana

Alternative names: Dandunwacana, Dandonwacana

Terms of address: Senopati

Type: Demon

Origin: Java

Description in the Javanese wayang retellings: One of the demons of Amarta who is defeated by the Pandawa. When he dies, he reincarnates into the body of Werkudara.

Notes on the Indian retellings: Not mentioned in the Indian Mahabharata.

Found in the follwing stories (lakon)


  1. Babad_Wanamarta

Family relationships


Siblings: Yudhistira, Dananjaya, Suparta
