Yamawidura

Terms of address: Raden

Type: Human

Origin: India

Description in the Sanskrit version: Vidura is the prime minister in the court of Hastinapura. He is born to Veda Vyasa and one of the maid-servants in the Kuru. He is viewed as a wise man who provides strong support to the Pandavas for the righteousness although he is often placed in a tough spot due to his royal allegiance to the Kuru house where the Kauravas reside and to his sense of consciousness when he witnesses many instances of unfair treatment towards the Pandavas.

Description in the Javanese version: Son of Abyasa and Datri. His half brother are Pandu and Drestarasta. He marries Dewi Pamarini [daughter of Praby Dipacandra] has two sons: Sanjaya dan Yuyutsuh. // satu ayah dengan Pandu dan Destrarastra

Found in the follwing lakon: Kunthi_Pilih_(Lahiripun_Adipati_Karna)
