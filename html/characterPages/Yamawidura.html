Yamawidura

Terms of address: Batara

Kalangan: Human

Description: Son of Abyasa and Datri. His half brother are Pandu and Drestarasta. He marries Dewi Pamarini [daughter of Praby Dipacandra] has two sons: Sanjaya dan Yuyutsuh.
