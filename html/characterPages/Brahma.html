Brahma

Terms of address: Batara

Type: God

Origin: India

Description in the Javanese wayang retellings: God of fire and teacher of Baladewa. He descended to the world of the mortals [ngejawantah] and reigned the kingdom of Gilingwesi after Watugunung passed away.

Notes on the Indian retellings: Brahma is the son of Vishnu. He emerges out of his navel seated on a lotus. His consort is Saraswati, goddess of education.

Found in the follwing stories (lakon)


  1. Wisanggeni_Lahir
  2. Gatotkaca_Lahir
