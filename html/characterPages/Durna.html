Durna

Alternative names: Kumbayana, Druna, Drona

Terms of address: Begawan, Resi

Type: Human

Origin: India

Notes on the Indian versions: Drona is the guru of both the Pandavas and Kauravas. One of his reasons for teaching the Pandavas and Kauravas was to create a strong force to defeat his enemy, King Drupada.

Description in the Javanese wayang versions: A religious man from Atasangin. He was a teacher to both Pandawa and Korawa. He was killed by [Drestadyumna], the son of his life-long enemy [Drupada].
