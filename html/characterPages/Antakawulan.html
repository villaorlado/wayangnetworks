Antakawulan

Alternative names: Rara_Wangen, Antarakawulan

Terms of address: Dewi

Type: Human

Origin: Java

Description in the Javanese wayang retellings: Wife of Prabu Barata, king of Ayodya. She had an affair with Arjuna.

Notes on the Indian retellings: The wife of Barata in India is called Sunanda.

Found in the follwing stories (lakon)


  1. Semar_Boyong_(Wahyu_Katetreman)
