Antakawulan

Alternative names: Rara_Wangen, Antarakawulan

Terms of address: Dewi

Type: Human

Origin: Java

Notes on the Indian versions: The wife of Barata in India is called Sunanda.

Description in the Javanese wayang versions: Wife of Prabu [Barata], king of Ayodya. She had an affair with [Arjuna].
