Bomanarakasura

Alternative names: Asura Naraka, Sitija

Terms of address: Kesatria

Kalangan: Human

Description: Also known as Asura Naraka. Son of Kresna (Wisnu) and Pertiwi. Known as Sitija in his youth. After fighting the demon Bomantara, he changes his name and absorbs some of the qualities of his erstwhile enemy. Brother of Siti Sendari. He is killed by Kresna (incarnation of Wisnu). Basudewa dies when Narakasura invades Mandura.

Father: Kresna

Siblings: SitiSendari, Godhayitma
