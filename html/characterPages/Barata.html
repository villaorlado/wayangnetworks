Barata

Terms of address: Prabu

Type: Human

Origin: India

Notes on the Sanskrit version: Son of King Dasharata and Queen Kaikeyi. His brothers are Rama, Lakshmana and Shatrughuna.

Description in the Javanese version: He becomes king of Ayodya when Rama is exiled to the forest. There is another Barata in the Mahabharata. This Barata is a son of Duswanta and Sakuntala and an ancestor of Bisma (12 generations up the family tree).

Family relationships

Mother: Kekayi

Father: Destarastra

Siblings: Satrugna, Kawakwa, Rama, Lesmana

More information

Sources: Ensiklopedi Wayang Purwa, p. 83.; Mengenal Gambar Tokoh Wayang Purwa, p. 38.

Found in the follwing lakon: Semar_Boyong_(Wahyu_Katetreman)
