Wilawuk

Alternative names: Wilwuk

Terms of address: Begawan

Type: Demon

Origin: Java

Notes on the Indian versions: Not mentioned in the Indian Mahabharata

Description in the Javanese wayang versions: He can take the shape of a winged snake. He gives a special weapon to Arjuna (Minyak Jayengkaton). He is the favter of Asmarawati, who is somtes said to be another name of Dewi Jimambang, who dreams of marrying Arjuna. Arjuna gives Wilawuk's cup to his siblings so they can see the invisible demons. There are differnet version about Jimambang. According to Babad Wanamarta, she is the daughter of Begawan WilawukThe Ensiklopedi Wayang Purwa describes Asmarawati as the daughter of Prabu [Suryaasmara] and the wife of Bambang [Sumitra], Arjuna's son. The same source describes Jim_Mambang as a separate character.

Found in the follwing stories (lakon):

  1. Babad_Wanamarta
