<html><head><title>Brajadhenta</title></head><br/><h1>Brajadhenta</h1></p><p><b>Kalangan</b>: Raksasa<p><b>Description</b>: He is the third son of Prabu Arimbaka (king of Pringgandani) and Dewi Hadimba. His siblings include Arima/Hidimba, Arimbi, Brajamusti, Brajalamatan, Brajawikalpa and Kalbendana. He opposes Gatotkaca becoming king of Pringgandani, and dies in armed conflict against him.
