<p><b>Description</b>: He is the third son of Prabu Arimbaka (king of Pringgandani) and Dewi Hadimba. His siblings include Arima/Hidimba, Arimbi, Brajamusti, Brajalamatan, Brajawikalpa and Kalbendana. He opposes Gatotkaca becoming king of Pringgandani, and dies in armed conflict against him. His brothers Brajalamatan and Brajawikalpa suffer the same fate.
