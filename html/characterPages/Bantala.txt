<p><h1>Bantala</h1></p><p><b>Terms of address</b>: Kala<p><b>Type</b>: Raksasa<p><b>Origin</b>: Java<p><b>Alternative names</b>: One of the brothers of Wibisana in /Wahyu Makutharama/. He represents the earth, and is one of several characters that constitute anthropomorphic representations of natural elements.
