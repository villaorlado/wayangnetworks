Rukmini

Terms of address: Dewi

Type: Human

Origin: India

Notes on the Indian versions: Rukmini is the first wife of Lord Krishna. She is known as one of his principle wives of Lord Krishna. In the Indian version, their first-born, Pradyumna, became the crowned Prince of Dwaraka.

Description in the Javanese wayang versions: First wife of Kresna. Daughter of Bismaka (Aya Prabu Rukma) and Rumbini. Has sibligs: Arya Rumkana and Dewi Rarasati (Larasati) -> this one from Ken Sagupi and Arya Prabu Rukma. Kresna (Narayana), rejecting Drona. The other wives of Kresna are Jembawati (daughter of Jembawan and Trijata), Setyaboma (daughter of Ugrasena / Setyajit, king of Lesmanapura). She has three sons: Saranadewa (face of giant), Partadewa and Dewi Sitisari / Titisari.

Found in the follwing stories (lakon):

  1. Narayana_Kridha_Brata
  2. Wisanggeni_Lahir
  3. Wahyu_Topeng_Waja
