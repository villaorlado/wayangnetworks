Bendana

Alternative names: Kalakatung

Terms of address: Kala

Type: Raksasa

Origin: Java

Notes on the Indian versions: Not mentioned in the Indian Mahabharata.

Description in the Javanese wayang versions: He is the son of Arimbaka (also known as Trimbaka or Trembaka in the Surakarta tradition). Kala Bendana was very fond of the young Gatotkaca. However, Kala Bendana was the one who told Dewi [Utari] that Abimanyu was seeking [Siti_Sundari]'s hand in marriage. As a result of this, [Utari] laid a course on Abimanyu. When this happened, Gatotkaca was so angered that he killed Kala Bendana. Bendana avenged his death when his spirit helped Karna kill Gatotkaca during the Bartayuda war. In the Yogyakarta, Kala Bendana and Kala Katung are separate characters.
