Baladewa

Terms of address: Prabu

Type: Human

Origin: India

Notes on the Sanskrit version: Known as Balarama, he is the elder brother of Krishna. He is also believed to be the reincarnation of Adisesha (The Snake who function as the bed for Lord Vishnu to rest on). He is known to have taught Bhima and Duryodhana the art of fighting with the mace. He is often depicted carrying a plough as well.

Alternative names: Wasi Jaladara, Kakrasana, Karsana, Kusuma Walikita, Curiganata

Description in the Javanese version: Brother of Kresna, also known as Kakrasana, and Wai Jaladara after becoming pendeta.

Family relationships

Mother: Rohini

Father: Basudewa

Siblings: Kresna
