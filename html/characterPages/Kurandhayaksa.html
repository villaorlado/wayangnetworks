Kurandhayaksa

Alternative names: Kurandageni

Terms of address: Prabu

Type: Raksasa

Origin: Java

Notes on the Indian versions: Not mentioned in the Indian Mahabharata.

Description in the Javanese wayang versions: The king of Tirtakandasan (also known as Tirtakandasar), a kingdom at the bottom of the sea.
