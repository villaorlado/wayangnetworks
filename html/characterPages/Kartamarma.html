Kartamarma

Terms of address: Raden

Type: Human

Origin: India

Notes on the Indian versions: In India he is not a Korawa.

Description in the Javanese wayang versions: He is the only one of the 100 Korawa who survives the Baratayuda war. He is killed by Werkudara after [Aswatama] infiltrates the Pandawa camp. In Java, there is a belief that he reincarnated as a cekakak bird (javan kingfisher) and that his chirps indicate the presence of thief. He is often used for the budhalan scene (departure of the troops) when this involves the Korawa.
