Duryudana

Terms of address: Prabu

Type: Human

Origin: India

Notes on the Sanskrit version: He is oldest son of the Dhirtirashtra and Gandhari. He learnt the art of mace fighting from Balarama and is believed to be his favourite.

Description in the Javanese version: He is the oldest of the Kurawa, son of Gandari and Destarastra. He marries Banowati and is the father of Lesmana Mandrakumara.
