Erawati

Terms of address: Dewi

Kalangan: Human

Description: One of the wives of Baladewa, daughter of Salya and Setyawati (Pujawati). She has four siblings: Surtikanti (who marries Karna), Burisrawa (face of giant), Rukmarata.

Mother: Setyawati

Father: Salya

Spouses: Baladewa

Ruler of: Surtikanti,Burisrawa,Rukmarata
