Indra

Terms of address: Batara

Type: God

Origin: India

Notes on the Sanskrit version: Known as Indra or Devendra, the King of Devas (celestial beings). His wife is known as Indrani. Indra is also mentioned the Krishna leela (the divine acts of Krishna) whereby the the fellow villagers of Lord Krishna used to perform prayers to propitiate Lord Indra for rain. However at one point of time when Krishna tells them not to do so, the villagers stop and this enrages Indra. He punishes the villages by causing stormy rains. However Krishna protects the villagers in the end.

Alternative names: Surapati, Diwapati

Description in the Javanese version: King of heaven. Controller of the weather. Often known as God of war. Also known as Diwapati.
