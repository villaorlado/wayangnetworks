Bagong

Alternative names: Bawor, Carub, Astrajingga
Surakarta Style. From Ki Catur Kuncoro's Collection.
Yogyakarta style. From Ki Cerma Sutejo's collection.

Type: Human

Origin: Java

Notes on the Indian versions: Not found in the Indian Mahabharata

Description in the Javanese wayang versions: Bagong was crafted from Semar's shadow by Sang Hyang Tunggal.
