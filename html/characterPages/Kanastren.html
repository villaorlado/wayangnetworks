Kanastren

Terms of address: Nyai

Kalangan: Bidadari

Description: Kanastren and Kaniraras are the consorts of Semar. They get them after Manumayasa shots two deers. He marries Kaniraras and Semar takes Kanastren.

Found in the follwing lakon : ['Semar mBangun Kayangan', 'Semar Boyong (Wahyu Katetreman)']
