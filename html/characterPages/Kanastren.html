Kanastren

Alternative names: Kanastri, Ganastri

Terms of address: Batari

Type: God

Origin: Java

Notes on the Indian versions: Not mentioned in the Indian Mahabharata.

Description in the Javanese wayang versions: Once Semar was being chased by two tigers. He found a hermit, [Manumayasa] and asked for his help. The hermit killed the tigers but in their deaths they became two beautiful women. Semar married one of them, Kanastren. And [Manumayasa] married the other one, [Kaniraras].

Found in the follwing stories (lakon):

  1. Semar_Boyong_(Wahyu_Katetreman)
  2. Semar_mBangun_Kayangan

Family relationships

Consorts: Semar

Offspring: Kamajaya, Wungkuam, Tambora, Wrahaspati, Siwah, Kuwera, Candra, Yamadipati, Surya, Darmanesti
