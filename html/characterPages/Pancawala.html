Pancawala

Terms of address: Raden

Type: Human

Origin: Java

Notes on the Indian versions: Pancawala does not exist in the Indian Mahabarata, where Draupadi is married to all of the Pandavas and bears sons from all of them. Prathivindhya is known as the son of Yudhishtra and Draupadi. He is also known as one of the /upapandavas/ (sons born to each of the Pandavas and Draupadi).

Description in the Javanese wayang versions: He marries Pergiwati, even though [Druna] tries to prevent this and marry her off to Duryudana's son Lesmana_Mandrakumara. He is killed by [Aswatama] in an attack after the end of the Bharatayuda. [Srikandi] and [Drestajumena] die in the same attack.
