Drestarastra

Terms of address: Prabu

Kalangan: Human

Description: Destarata. Son of Wicitrawirya's widow Ambika (who got pregnant from an upacara). Pandu is his younger 'brother' (son of Ambalika), as well as Widura. In Javanese version, he is the biological son of Abyasa. With Gandari he becomes the father of the Korawa.

Found in the follwing lakon : ['Babad Wanamarta']
