Bantala

Terms of address: Kala

Type: Raksasa

Origin: Java

Notes on the Sanskrit version: Not mentioned in the Indian Mahabharata.

Alternative names: One of the brothers of Wibisana in /Wahyu Makutharama/. He represents the earth, and is one of several characters that constitute anthropomorphic representations of natural elements.
