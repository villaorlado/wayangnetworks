Maerah

Alternative names: Amirah, Mahira, Maekah

Terms of address: Dewi

Type: Human

Origin: India

Description in the Javanese wayang retellings: She is married to Basudewa. But Gorawangsa impersonates Basudewa and seduces her. Basudewa sentences her to death and tasks Bismaka with her execution. However, upon learning that she is pregnant, Bismaka cannot get himself to kill her, and he abandons her in the forest instead. She is discovered and protected by resi [Anggawangsa], who brings her to Wisarengga, where she gives birth to Kangsa. As an adult Kangsa rebels against Basudewa but is killed by Baladewa. After his defeat, Maerah commits ritual suicide.

Notes on the Indian retellings: Not mentioned in the Indian Mahabharata.
