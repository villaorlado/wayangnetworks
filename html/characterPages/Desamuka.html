Desamuka

Alternative names: Dasamuka, Rahwana

Terms of address: Prabu

Type: Raksasa

Origin: India

Notes on the Indian versions: Dasamukha is another name for Ravana in the Ramayana. It means "10 faces."

Description in the Javanese wayang versions: He is the antagonist in the Ramayana.
