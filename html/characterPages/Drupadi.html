Drupadi

Terms of address: Dewi

Type: Human

Origin: India

Notes on the Sanskrit version: Known as Draupadi. She is also known as Panchali. She is considered an ayonija (she who is not born out of a mother) as she emerged out of the /yagna/ fire. She is therefore considered powerful and pure. She is married to all five Pandhava in the Indian version.

Alternative names: Dewi_Kresna

Description in the Javanese version: She was born to [Drupada] without the intervention of a woman, but by the works of Resi Yoja and Upajoya. In the Javanese Pedhalangan version, she is only married to Puntadewa and has only one son: Pancawala. She wears no golden accessories. Her character is simple and loyal.

Found in the follwing lakon (stories):
