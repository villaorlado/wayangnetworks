Dewaki

Terms of address: Dewi

Type: Human

Origin: India

Description in the Sanskrit version: Known as Devaki, the princess of Mathura. After her marriage with Vasudeva, the aakashavani (divine prophecy from the skies) announces that Kamsa (her brother) will die at the hands of her eight child. Upon hearing this, Kamsa imprisons both Devaki and Vasudewa and kills every child born to them. He is, however unsuccessful in killing the 7th and 8th child of the couple.

Description in the Javanese version: Mother of Kresna

Spouses: Basudewa

Offspring: Kresna

Found in the follwing lakon: Semar_Barang_Jantur, Basudewa_Grogol
