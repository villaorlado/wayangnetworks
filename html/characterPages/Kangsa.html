Kangsa

Terms of address: Prabu

Type: Human

Origin: India

Description in the Sanskrit version: A brother of Devaki. After Devaki marries Vasudeva, the aakashavani (divine prophecy from the skies) announces that Kamsa will die at the hands of her eight child. Upon hearing this, Kamsa imprisons both Devaki and Vasudewa and kills every child born to them. He is, however unsuccessful in killing the 7th and 8th child of the couple.

Alternative names: Jaka Maruta

Description in the Javanese version: Son of Gorawangsa and Maerah.

Mother: Maerah

Father: Gorawangsa

Found in the follwing lakon: Basudewa_Grogol
