Nakula

Alternative names: Pinten

Terms of address: Raden

Type: Human

Origin: India

Notes on the Indian versions: He is the originally the son of the Ashwini twins and Kunti. He is believed to be the most handsome amongst the Pandavas.

Description in the Javanese wayang versions: Although nominally the son of Pandu, he is in reality an incarnation of Aswin. After the Baratayuda war, he became the rules of Mandaraka. He was an able horseman. He died a perfect death with his four brothers some time after that.
