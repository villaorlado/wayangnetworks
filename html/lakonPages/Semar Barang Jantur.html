8. :Mandaraka,Tirta Kadhasar. Salya has organized a contest, with a reward for whomever can return Erawati. If the winner is a man, he can take Erawati in marriage. If the winner is a woman, she will be considered as a sister of Erawati. Arjuna and Baladewa soon arrive. At night, they hide to see if Erawati’s thief will return for Surtikanthi. This is in fact what happens and the thief is none other than Kartapiyoga. When they see him capture Surtikanthi, they follow him back to Tirta Kadhasar. Once there, they hide Surtikanthi and Erawati inside of a ring. But Kartapiyoga sees them and tries to stop them. Arjuna kills Kartapiyoga with a spear. Kurandhayaksa then tries to seek vengeance but is in turn also killed by Arjuna’s arrow. Baladewa and Arjuna then return to Mandaraka.
