This lakon has 26 characters: Bomanarakasura, LesmanaMandrakumara, Durna, Karna, Banowati, Sadewa, Sengkuni, Bilung, Puntadewa, Semar, Antaboga, Arjuna, Werkudara, Gatotkaca, Sri, Petruk, Baladewa, Duryudana, Nakula, Sadana, Togog, Antareja, Anoman, Kresna, Godhayitma, Abimanyu,

1.Court:Astina Duryudana talks with Durna, Sengkuni and Karna – king of Ngawangga. LesmanaMandrakumara wants to marry the daughter of Togog, called RaraTemon. Karna is sent to Parangtejo, followed by the Korawa.

2.Kedaton:Astina. Duryudana tells Banowati that he has sent Karna to Togog’s residence. Banowati and Duryudana eat and then Duryudana shreds his royal garments for his religious robes. He enters his meditation chambers and asks the gods to support his plan.

3.Alun-alun:Astina. Sengkuni instructs the Korawa to follow Karna to Parangteja. He stresses the importance of the mission.

4.Village:Parangteja. Togog is taking care of Bilung, who is sick. Karna and Petruk arrive simultaneously, both with the intention of asking for the hand of RaraTemon. They fight and exit the chambers. Togog asks RaraTemon for her opinion. RaraTemon says she will marry whomever can bring several special gifts, including a golden chariot pulled by a giant Garuda with a dragon as whip.

5.Village:Parangteja. Petruk and Anoman, who is accompanying him as a sentinel, are attacked by the Korawa, who are led by Sengkuni. Togog breaks up the fight and explains RaraTemon’s conditions. Both Karna and Petruk then return to their houses, bearing news of the contest.

6.Village:Karangkadempel. Semar, Arjuna, Werkudara, Puntadewa, Nakula, Sadewa, Baladewa and Kresna. Petruk arrives and communicates Togog’s message. This makes Semar sad. Puntadewa instructs the Pandawa to search for the chariot. Abimanyu and Gatotkaca will go to Trajustrisna and Antareja will go to Saptapratala.

7.Forest:Unnamed. Abimanyu fights the WadyaTawanggantungan, from a country ruled by Godhayitma.

8.Sabrangan:Trajutrisna. Bomanarakasura receives Godhayitma. Abimanyu and Gatotkaca arrive and explain their mission. Godhayitma becomes angry and insults Bomanarakasura. They fight and Godhayitma loses. He leaves, insulting Bomanarakasura.

9. Kayangan:Saptapratala. Antareja explains to Antaboga that Semar wants to marry JakaPupon to RaraTemon and needs Antaboga’s help for this. Antaboga gladly agrees to help.

10. Forest:UnnamedKarna and Sengkuni stop Gatotkaca and Antareja, who have already secured the gifts. Antareja and Gatotkaca display their anger, sending the Korawa running for their lives.

11. Village:Karangkadempel. Abimanyu, Gatotkaca and Antareja arrive in Semar’s house, bringing the necessary gifts with them. JakaPupon leaves with them in the direction of Parangteja.

12. Village:Parangteja. Togog receives Semar, Abimanyu, Gatotkaca, Antareja, Kresna and Baladewa. JakaPupon becomes Sadana and RaraTemon becomes Sri. /
