This lakon has 25 characters: Samba, Guru, LesmanaMandrakumara, Durna, Karna, Udawa, Banowati, Sadewa, Sengkalawati, Sengkuni, Puntadewa, Semar, Arjuna, Werkudara, WulanDremi, WulanDrema, Duryudana, Nakula, Narada, Setyaki, Kartamarma, Indra, Anoman, Kresna, Abimanyu,

1. Jejer:Astina. Duryudana faces Karna, Durna , Sengkuni and Kartamarma. They discuss a Nugraha from the gods, which will allow the young generations to hold on to power in the future. Durna instructs the Adipati of Astina, LesmanaMandrakumara, to obtain this Nugraha which will fall in the Kendrawahana forest. Duryudana agrees to this. Durna, Karna and Sengkuni are ordered to accompany him.

2. Kedatonan:Astina. Banowati awaits the return of king Duryudana. She washes his feet. Duryudana says that their son wants to receive the Wahyu Cakraningrat. After eating, the king enters his meditation chambers to ask for the favor of the gods.

3. Alun-alun:Astina. Durna accompanies his grandson LesmanaMandrakumara on the Kreta Kencana, together with Karna, Sengkuni and the Korawa who are headed to the Kendrawahana forest.

4. :Dwarawati. Kresna exits his chambers and meets Setyaki and Samba. Kresna gives them advice about how a king should behave in front of the common folk. The gods are ready to give the Wahyu and Samba is ready to go in its pursuit. He will be accompanied in his mission by Anoman.

5. Kendrawahana:Forest. Setyaki, Anoman, Samba and Udawa meet Durna and the Korawa. A fight ensues but they carry on their own ways.

6. Kendrawahana:Forest. Abimanyu is searching for the Nugraha. He is accompanied by the true god of Java, Semar. After Abimanyu finishes his meditation, they meet the WadyaBalaDhandhangMangore. A fight ensues and Abimanyu is able to defeat them and carry on his meditation.

7. :Jonggringsaloka. Guru, Narada, and Indra receive WulanDrema and WulanDremi. On Sang Hyang Guru's command, these two Gods want to become the seed of Javanese kings. Therefore, they must soon descend to the word of the mortals to become one with a warrior which will one day be strong enough to be the father of kings. After asking for permission and kissing the feet of Guru, these two gods descend to Marcapada to search for the right receptacle of the Wahyu Cakraningrat.

8. Jejer:AmartaWerkudara, Puntadewa, Arjuna, Nakula and Sadewa receive Kresna. They talk about the Nugraha which will choose a young man as receptacle and enable him to reign over the world. Kresna invites Arjuna to accompany Abimanyu and Samba. They depart.

9. Kendrawahana:Forest. Even though LesmanaMandrakumara is the son of a king, he is not used to meditation. He possesses no wisdom and no resolve. But the Carkaningrat also wants to enter his body. As WulanDrema enters his body he faints. But is then successfully tempted by a a woman named Endang Sengkalawati. Therefore, the WahyuCakraningrat exits his body.

10. Kendrawahana:Forest. Samba is not far from LesmanaMandrakumara. The Wahyu Cakraningrat also enters his heart. As a gift for his meditation, Samba receives the gift. But he is also tempted by Sengkalawati's breasts and the Wahyu exits his body, flying into the firmament.

11. Kendrawahana:Forest. Abimanyu has practiced meditation and feels at ease in his heart. He is accompanied by Semar. Even though his body is ugly, Semar is also a god. Therefore, WulanDrema feels at home in Abimanyu's body. WulanDrema takes leave from his wife WulanDremi. They will meet again in Warata. Since Abimanyu is not tempted, he will return to Amarta. But in the road he meets Arjuna, Kresna and Samba and they all return to Amarta. The Korawa move in an angry dance. Werkudara sends them in all directions. The Pandawa thank the havens for the Wahyu they've received./
