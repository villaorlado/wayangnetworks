Semar_Mantu

This lakon has 18 characters: Gatotkaca, Sengkuni, Samba, Bisawarna, Gareng, Petruk, Baladewa, Bagong, Dhadhungawuk, Setyaki, Anoman, Abimanyu, Kresna, Semar, Siti_Sendari, Arjuna, Duryudana, Werkudara,

1. Court Dwarawati. Kresna, Samba and Setyaki, await the arrival of Baladewa. Duryudhana had asked Baladewa for help in order to get RaraTemon to marry Lesmana_Mandrakumara. As this is being explained, Petruk arrives and asks for a similar thing: help get RaraTemon married to Senet. Kresna is confused about what to do.

2. Kedaton Dwarawati. Kresna meets his daughter RaraTemon. She wants to know who she will marry. Kresna decides to set a series of difficult conditions for his future son in law, including a golden cart pulled by giant horses, one hundred and forty wild buffaloes with white legs and a white monkey that can dance.

3. Alun-alun Dwarawati. Petruk,Gareng and Bagong are attacked by the Korawa, but Anoman helps them. Baladewa arrives and breaks the fight. They all go back to their respective places.

4. Astina. Duryudana receives Baladewa, who explains the conditions of the marriage, then distributes orders to find the gifts.

5. Village Karangkadempel. Petruk arrives and explains Karnaâ€™s conditions to Semar, who is shocked. Semar instructs the Pandawa to help. Werkudara, Gatotkaca and Arjuna leave to find the gifts.

6. Alun-alun Singgelapura. The RatuSewuNagari are gathered, planning to attack and defeat Bisawarna. But Werkudara defeats the RatuSewuNagari. Bisawarna allows Werkudara to borrow the golden cart pulled by giant horses.

7. Forest Krendhawahana. Gatotkaca takes one hundred and forty buffaloes from Dhadhungawuk, who takes care of them for Indra. Arjuna brings Anoman, since he is a white monkey that can dance.

8. Court Astina The Korawa try and fail to secure the same gifts and are angry at the Pandawa for their successes. Sengkuni provides a false report to Baladewa, who becomes angry.

9. Village Karangkadempel. Semar receives a visit from Werkudara, Gatotkaca and Arjuna, all bearing the proper gifts.

10. Court Dwarawati. Kresna awaits the arrival of Semar, Werkudara, Gatotkaca and Arjuna. Senet becomes Abimanyu and RaraTemon becomes Siti_Sendari, making Baladewa embarrassed.
