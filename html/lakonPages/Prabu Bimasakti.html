Prabu Bimasakti

This lakon has 21 characters: Gatotkaca, Surabramadiraja, Sengkuni, Baladewa, Puntadewa, GagakBaka, Antareja, Semar, Durna, Nakula, Arimbi, Gareng, Anoman, Bagong, Kresna, Petruk, Arjuna, Duryudana, Werkudara, Sadewa, Antasena,

1. Court Astina. Duryudana, Durna, Sengkuni and Kartamarma receive Baladewa. Duryudana wants to borrow a weapon from Baladewa in order to defeat Werkudara, who has become king of Randhugumbala. Baladewa feels well treated and decides to accept their request. He is followed by the Korawa and Sengkuni.

2. Kedaton Astina. Banowati awaits the return of Duryudana. He soon arrives and has food with his wife. After having food, he puts on his religious robes and makes an offer to the Gods, so that his plan to defeat Werkudara can work.

3. Alun-alun Astina. Sengkuni instructs the Korawa to follow the instructions of the king and accompany Baladewa.

4. Court Randhungumbala. Bimasakti seats in his throne, receiving Anoman. Anoman originally had doubts about Bimasakti’s ability to reign but after hearing the full story, he now respects Bimasakti’s wisdom. The Korawa arrive, led by Baladewa and a fight ensues. Anoman, Antareja, Antasena and Gatotkaca are able to defeat the Korawa.

5. Sabrangan Simbarmanyura. The king Surabramadiraja has fallen in love with Arimbi. Even though he has been advised by his Patih against it, Surabramadiraja orders his army to get ready to capture Arimbi.

6. Forest Tikbrasara . Gatotkaca, Semar, Gareng, Bagong and Petruk. Gatotkaca wants to know about his father, Werkudara. They are surprised by WadyaRotadenawa an army of giants, who are promptly defeated by Gatotkaca, who then goes to Amarta.

7. Court Jodipati . Werkudara is outside his chambers. Arimbi is lost in thought and captured by Surabramadiraja. This is witnessed by GagakBaka, but he can’t pursue the kidnapper because he cannot fly.

8. Court Amarta. Puntadewa, Arjuna, Nakula and Sadewa receive Kresna. Gatotkaca arrives. Sengkuni also arrives suddenly, who says that Werkudara has been killed by Bimasakti. Gatotkaca hears that his father has been killed and leaves at once. Kresna and Puntadewa follow him.

9. Court Randhungumbala.Gatotkaca starts attacking everyone and is challenged by Antasena . Before an injury occurs, Kresna separates them. Kresna explains that Gatotkaca is the son of Werkudara. Puntadewa is given the Wahyu Kantreman by Sang Hyang Bimasakti that has taken the body of Bimasakti. Then Wenang returns to the heavens and Bimasakti becomes Werkudara. He is informed that Arimbi has been kidnapped. Gatotkaca flies away and is able to intercept Surabramadiraja, killing him and rescuing his mother Arimbi. The family of Werkudara is finally reunited and they all have food./
